Mashed Potatoes

Description

These mashed potatoes are too fattening to have on a regular basis. But they are perfect for the holidays. Everyone who eats them will wonder what's in them that makes them so rich.

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds red potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place potatoes in a large pot with enough water to cover.
  2. Bring to a boil, and cook until fork tender, about 20 minutes.
  3. Drain, add butter, and mash using a potato masher, ricer or mixer.
  4. Gradually beat in the heavy cream.
  5. Serve hot, and Enjoy!
